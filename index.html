JM Designs

Welcome! I'm Justine Wood. I'm originally from Minneapolis, Minnesota. I have always enjoyed being a visual person abut also be creative. I received my Bachelors of Science in Interior Design in 2007 for The Art Institute International. After, I moved to Nashville for my family and have been here for six years. While working in a field completely different from design, I found myself missing designing and creating.

In 2015, I decided I wanted to go back to school and get my Bachelors in Graphic Design. After looking at all my options, I decided to attend The Art Institute of TN Nashville campus. The degree they offer is Graphic and Web Design. I originally started school with the graphic concertation but was exacted to learn the web part. After taking my first web class, I was hooked and switched to the web concertation. December 2017, I will have my Bachelors in Graphic and Web designs with the emphasis on Web. After school, I will be a front-end web developer.
